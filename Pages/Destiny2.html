Discription

"Destiny 2" is a popular online multiplayer first-person shooter (FPS) game developed by Bungie. Initially released in 2017 for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Microsoft Windows, and later for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S, "Destiny 2" has evolved into a live service game known for its cooperative and competitive gameplay, expansive worlds, and ongoing narrative-driven content updates. Here’s an overview of what makes "Destiny 2" a significant title in the FPS genre.

Extreme Violence, Strong Language, Simulated Gambling

+Offers in-app purchases. Game requires online multiplayer subscription to play on console (Game Pass Core or Ultimate, sold separately).
